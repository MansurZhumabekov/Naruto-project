Uzumaki Naruto

7th Hokage of Hidden Leaf Village

Here is the most important events in Naruto's life to become stronger

  • Prologue and Land of Waves - We meet Naruto. He’s a practical joker, unpredictable and sort of a loser. Of course, the audience knows the truth, that Naruto is the orphaned son of a great leader, and that he has the powerful Nine-Tailed Fox trapped inside of him.

  • Chunin Exams arc - Quite a few major characters get introduced in this arc. There are some fun battles here. Everyone is competing for the coveted chunin prize, and not actually trying to kill each other. As a result, we get to see them show off some awesome ninja moves and clever strategies.

  • Konoha Crush - This arc is short, but important. Orochimaru attacks the Hidden Leaf village. He battles the Third Hokage.

  • Jiraiya, the Akatsuki and Sasuke Leaves The Search for Tsunade - Here we have the first time that Jiraiya teaches Naruto, as the two head out in search of Jiraiya’s former ninja teammate. We also have the first appearance of Sasuke’s brother, Itachi. Later on, his story will get a lot more complicated, but for now, he’s just a member of the evil Akatsuki.

  • Sasuke Recovery Mission - After a fight with Naruto, Sasuke leaves Konoha. He leaves because he wants to be strong enough to fight Itachi. Naruto has been making a lot of progress, and Sasuke feels like he hasn’t. Orochimaru offers Sasuke a deal, and he takes it.

  • Kazekage Rescue Mission - The first arc of Naruto Shippuden shows Gaara being targeted by the Akatsuki, who intend to extract the One-Tails Shukaku from him.

  • Tenchi Bridge Reconnaissance Mission - The Tenchi Bridge Reconnaissance Mission Arc returns to an all-new Team 7. The classic team now features a ninja by the name of Sai and is led by Kakashi Hatake, who feels guilty for letting his team down.

  • Akatsuki Suppression Mission - This story arc introduced the Akatsuki members Hidan and Kakazu. Hidan is both hated and loved for taking the life of Asuma Sarutobi.

  • Tale of Jiraiya the Gallant - This story arc followed Jiraiya as he discovered that Pain was actually Nagato—his first and former pupil. It tracked his journey to stop Nagato from attacking the Hidden Leaf Village.

  • Fated Battle Between Brothers - After Jiraiya's death, the next story arc dealt with Sasuke finding Itachi first and battling him to fight death. Although the battle was wonderful, Itachi's characterization is what most fans remember.

  • Pain's Assault - The Pain's Assault Arc featured the long-awaited battle between Naruto and Jiraiya's former student and killer, Pain—otherwise known as Nagato.

  • Five Kage Summit - The Five Kage Summit arc focuses sees Naruto acting more hopeless than usual. He wishes to ask the Kage not to execute Sasuke for his crimes.

  • Fourth Shinobi World War - The biggest war in history, which united all ninjas against Madara and Tobi.

  • Kaguya Otsutsuki Strikes - The Kaguya Otsutsuki Arc ran off and on for over 20 episodes, with various fillers and intermissions in between. Kaguya's first appearance shows the looming end to the war as well as her exciting backstory.

  • Konoha Hiden: The Perfect Day For A Wedding - Konoha Hiden: The Perfect Day For A Wedding ran from episode 494 until episode 500—the final episode of the Naruto Shippuden anime.
